Why does timing matter? 🤔

Getting pregnant too soon (short interpregnancy interval) doesn't give your body enough time to recover. Here is what we are protecting against:

👶

For the Baby

  • 📉 Preterm Birth: Arriving too early.
  • ⚖️ Low Birth Weight: Growing less than expected.
👩

For the Mom

  • 🏥 Uterine Rupture Risk: Especially if you've had a C-section.
  • 🔋 Nutritional Depletion: Your body needs to restock vitamins!
Critical Safety Info

The First 6 Weeks 🩸

Did you know? In the first 6 weeks after delivery, your risk of a blood clot is higher than at any other time.

Because of this, we avoid Estrogen products (like the regular pill) immediately after birth.

Safe Options (0-6 Weeks)
